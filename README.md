# Student Learning Performance Visual Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project applies visual analytics techniques to examine factors associated with student learning performance at the University of Education, Vietnam National University, Hanoi. Using a cleaned survey dataset of student and alumni responses, I built an interactive Tableau Story to explore respondent demographics, GPA distribution, student lifestyle patterns, parental background, and perceived institutional factors affecting academic outcomes.

**Tableau Public Dashboard:**  
https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/hann.danish7698/viz/HannDanishAssignment1/Story1

## Problem Statement
The objective was to reveal and communicate key observations from survey data using appropriate visual analytics methods. The analysis focused on three areas:

1. Respondent demographic profile  
2. Behavioural and perception-based survey responses  
3. Factors associated with GPA performance  

## Dataset
The dataset was based on an online questionnaire conducted between March and May 2023. The final cleaned dataset contained 2,170 responses. The survey included demographic variables, family background, student activities, GPA categories, and perception-based questions on university support, lecturer quality, facilities, curriculum, peer influence, and classroom competition.

## Key Insights
- The respondent sample was heavily female and graduate-skewed, which may introduce sample bias.
- Most respondents fell within the “Fair” and “Good” GPA categories.
- Comparing GPA percentages within gender was more meaningful than comparing raw counts because female respondents formed the majority of the sample.
- Students from non-minority, non-policy-supported, and non-poor-household backgrounds had a higher percentage of Good and Excellent GPA outcomes.
- Students with Excellent GPA were more likely to have parents with university-level education.
- Poor GPA respondents had a higher proportion of students spending more than four hours daily on social media.
- Lecturer quality was rated as one of the most important perceived factors affecting education outcomes.

## Visual Analytics Techniques Used
- Calculated fields for GPA labels
- Percentage-within-category calculations
- Pivoting survey variables for analysis
- Heatmaps for bivariate comparison
- Stacked bar charts for demographic and parental background profiling
- Dashboard filters for interactive factor comparison
- Tableau Story format for guided visual communication
